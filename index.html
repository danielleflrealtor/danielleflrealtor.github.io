New to Sarasota?

Everything I tell every client before they move

01 · The Suncoast Lifestyle

Life here revolves around the water, farmers markets, arts events, and beautiful weather. Sarasota is a cultural hub with a small-town feel — don't be surprised when you never want to leave.

02 · Cost of Living

Sarasota has grown significantly. Budget around $1,800–$2,800/mo for a 1–2 BR rental; homeownership starts around $280K in Bradenton and climbs quickly toward the water. Florida has no state income tax.

03 · Neighborhoods 101

Downtown Sarasota is walkable and vibrant. Lakewood Ranch offers master-planned community living with great schools. The keys (Siesta, Lido, Longboat) are beach living at its finest. Bradenton is more affordable and growing fast.

04 · Getting Around

You'll need a car — public transit is limited. Most locals live within 20 minutes of the beach. Traffic builds in season (Jan–April), so factor that into your commute planning.

05 · Working With a Realtor is FREE for Buyers

In Florida, the seller pays the buyer's agent commission — so working with me costs you absolutely nothing as a buyer. No reason not to have a local expert fully in your corner.
